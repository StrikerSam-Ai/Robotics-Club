Technical & Career Growth

  • Practical Skills: Go beyond textbooks and learn Arduino programming, AI/ML basics, mechanical design (CAD/MATLAB), and ROS in our hands-on workshops.
  • Build Your Portfolio: Work on impressive projects that will make you stand out to employers and graduate programs.
  • Mentorship: Get guidance and support from experienced senior students and faculty mentors.
  • Competition Experience: Participate in major events like RoboCup and ROBOCON to test your skills and compete on a national level.
  • Future-Ready: Develop into a dynamic innovator and problem-solver, a "future-ready engineer" prepared for a career in robotics and beyond.

About The Club

The Robotics Club at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati Campus, is a vibrant, multidisciplinary community of students passionate about robotics, AI, and automation. It is open to all disciplines and encourages collaboration and creativity. Founded in 2024, we provide a hands-on environment to learn, collaborate, and build.

Our Mission

The club's mission is encapsulated in its theme, "Innovate, Automate, and Elevate: Building the Future with AI for Robotics." This mission is designed to empower undergraduate students with the essential skills, knowledge, and creativity required to explore the dynamic field of robotics.

Our Vision

To be the premier campus hub where students transform theoretical knowledge into real-world robotic solutions, fostering a culture of practical innovation that prepares them to lead in a technology-driven world.
